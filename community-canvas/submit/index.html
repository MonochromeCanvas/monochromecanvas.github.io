Submissions are reviewed by the studio before they appear in the public gallery.

Send your paper piece to the gallery

Upload one image of artwork created on the recycled studio paper. We ask for an email so the studio can review and follow up, but you can choose what credit appears publicly.

What to include

  • One photo of one finished piece. Repeat submissions are welcome.
  • An email for studio follow-up. It is not displayed publicly.
  • Optional artist credit, social handle, website, or location.
  • Optional notes about the artwork or your experience with Monochrome Canvas.
  • Approved work may be shared by Monochrome Canvas with the credit you provide.
